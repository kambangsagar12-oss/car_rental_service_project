Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common car rental questions.

To hire a car with CarRental, you usually need a valid driving licence, proof of identity, and a valid payment method. Additional documents may be required depending on the vehicle type and booking location.

Basic protection may be included depending on your booking, and extra coverage options may also be available. Always review the rental terms carefully before confirming your reservation.

The minimum age to hire a car depends on the location and the type of vehicle. In many cases, drivers must be at least 21 years old, and younger drivers may have extra conditions or fees.

Drivers generally need a full and valid driving licence held for the required minimum period. Depending on where your licence was issued, additional identification or an international driving permit may be needed.

Debit card acceptance depends on the rental location, car type, and payment policy. Some bookings allow debit cards, while others may require a credit card for verification and security purposes.
